3. Layout & Interaction Rules

Layout

Main Dashboard uses a two-column shell: left fixed sidebar (260px) and right 12-column content grid.

  • Header spans 12 columns.
  • Room chips also span 12 columns under header.
  • AC card takes 4 columns; usage status takes the other 8 columns in the same row.
  • Devices and Light cards are placed in the next row (8 + 4 columns).
Interaction States
  • .chip.active – selected room.
  • .btn.active – primary action / selected filter.
  • .ac-toggle.on – AC power on.
  • .mode-btn.active – current AC mode.
  • .device-toggle.on – device power on.
  • .menu-item.active – current sidebar menu item.
